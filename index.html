ENHYPEN Doubles Their 1st-Week Sales Record + Tops Oricon’s Weekly Album Chart With “DIMENSION : DILEMMA”

ENHYPEN has set a new record with their first full album “DIMENSION : DILEMMA”!

On October 19, Hanteo Chart announced that “DIMENSION : DILEMMA” had sold 818,716 copies during its first week of release (October 12 to October 18).

The album had already sold 501,000 copies on October 12, the day the album dropped, and approximately 300,000 more copies were sold during the six days after that.

ENHYPEN has set the record for the highest first-week sales for a group that debuted in 2020. Their previous album “BORDER : CARNIVAL,” released in April, had sold 384,699 copies in its first week. The new figure achieved by “DIMENSION : DILEMMA” is more than double their previous record, reflecting ENHYPEN’s rapid growth.

The swollen part under your eye will be gone the morning you wake up and your dark circles will become lighter. You can also apply this product on any part of your face and it is water soluble so you can mix it with warm water and transform it into a brightening serum.
